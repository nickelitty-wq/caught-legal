Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Caught - Self Improvement respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Caught does not collect personally identifiable information. All app data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Locally stored data may include:

  • Activity check-in entries (category + timestamp)
  • App settings (wake/sleep time, notification frequency)
  • Onboarding completion status

Subscription and Payment Data

Caught uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat and Apple may collect information required to process payments, such as purchase history, device identifiers, and subscription status.

Caught does not have access to your full payment details. All transactions are handled securely through the App Store.

You can review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy here: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Permissions

Caught requests permission to send push notifications to deliver your random check-in prompts. This permission is optional and can be managed in your device settings at any time. Without notifications, the core check-in loop will not function.

Data Security

All check-in data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Your Rights and Choices

You may delete your locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the app. For subscription-related data, you may manage your information through your Apple ID account settings.

Children's Privacy

Caught is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
