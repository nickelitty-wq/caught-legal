About Caught

Caught sends you random check-ins throughout the day asking one simple question: what are you doing right now? At the end of the week, you see a breakdown of how you actually spent your time — not how you think you did.

Most people think they're productive. Caught shows you the truth.

Features

  • Random push notification check-ins
  • One-tap activity logging
  • Weekly time breakdown report
  • Shareable weekly insights
  • Full check-in history
  • Local data storage — no account required
